About the Department

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Founded in 1970, the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering has established itself as a centre of academic and research excellence. We nurture talent through rigorous curriculum, state-of-the-art laboratories, and strong industry partnerships

Our curriculum spans analog and digital electronics, signal processing, wireless communications, embedded systems, and VLSI design — preparing graduates for roles in core engineering, research, and technology leadership.

With over four decades of distinguished history, we drive innovation through funded research centres, nationally recognised projects, and a growing network of alumni across leading organisations worldwide.

Research-Driven Learning

Students engage with active research from day one, guided by leading academics and industry experts.

Industry Partnerships

Deep collaboration with leading technology firms and startups for internship and placement opportunities.

Flexible Pathways

Undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programmes tailored to diverse career goals and aspirations.
